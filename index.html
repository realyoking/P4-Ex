Reading Passage: A Visit to Ocean Park

Last Sunday, my family visited Ocean Park in Hong Kong. It was a bright and sunny day. We rode the cable car up the hill. From the top, we saw the sparkling sea and tall green trees all around us. The park was busy with happy visitors. First, we went to see the giant pandas. They were eating fresh bamboo and rolling playfully on the grass. The pandas looked very cute and relaxed. My little sister wanted to hug one, but the sign said it was not allowed. Next, we watched the exciting dolphin show. The smart dolphins jumped high out of the water and played with colourful balls. They waved their tails at the crowd. Everyone clapped and cheered because the show was wonderful. We also visited an exhibition about ocean pollution. There were pictures of dirty beaches and animals hurt by plastic rubbish. The guide explained that we must throw away rubbish correctly and recycle to protect sea turtles, fish and other creatures. At the end of the day, we bought souvenirs and took many photos. I felt tired but very happy. The visit taught me to care for nature and keep our environment clean. I hope we can return to Ocean Park soon.
